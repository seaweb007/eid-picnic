FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Eid Picnic 2027.

Where is the venue?

Garden Opposite Government House, Alagbaka, Akure, Ondo State.

How do I reserve my place?

Visit our Tickets page to see all pass options, or click the 'Reserve via WhatsApp' button and send us a message. We'll guide you through the process.

Can children attend?

Absolutely! Eid Picnic is family-friendly with a dedicated Kids Corner — supervised play, face painting, and activities for all ages.

Can I become a vendor?

Yes! We welcome food, product, and creative vendors. Visit our Vendors page or send us a message on WhatsApp and we'll share booth options.

How do I become a sponsor?

We'd love to partner with you. Check out our Sponsors page or reach out on WhatsApp and we'll send you our sponsorship deck.

How do I volunteer?

Visit our Volunteers page or send us a message on WhatsApp to express interest, and we'll share available roles.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothing, a positive spirit, and any personal items you might need.

Is there parking?

Yes, there is ample parking available at the Government House Gardens venue.

What's the dress code?

Smart casual. Come comfortable and ready to enjoy the day — traditional Eid attire is always welcome!

Are tickets refundable?

Reach out to us directly on WhatsApp about your specific reservation and we'll do our best to help.

Is the event accessible?

Yes, the venue is on flat, open ground. Let us know in advance on WhatsApp if you or a family member need specific accommodations.
