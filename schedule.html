Schedule

The day at a glance

From morning prayer to evening lights — a full day of connection and celebration.

Everything runs on Eid Day (date confirmed after moon sighting).

8:00 AM

Gates Open & Eid Prayer

Welcome to Eid Picnic! Start the day with a communal prayer.

8:30 AM

Breakfast & Check-in

Grab a light breakfast, pick up your welcome package, and get settled in.

9:00 AM

Photo Booth Opens

Capture your first moments with family and friends.

10:00 AM

Games Begin

Basketball, football, board games, and more — the action starts.

11:00 AM

Kids Zone Opens

Face painting, crafts, and supervised play for the little ones.

12:00 PM

Food Court Opens

Explore a curated selection of local vendors.

1:00 PM

Sip & Paint Session

Unwind with a guided painting experience.

2:00 PM

Family Quiz

Test your knowledge in a fun, family-friendly quiz.

3:00 PM

Networking Hour

Connect with professionals, creatives, and community leaders.

4:00 PM

Live Entertainment

Music, performances, and special guest appearances.

5:00 PM

Giveaways & Awards

Exciting prizes and recognition for outstanding community members.

6:00 PM

Closing & Group Photos

Come together for a final celebration and group photo.

7:00 PM

Event Concludes

Thank you for being part of Eid Picnic 2027. See you next year!
