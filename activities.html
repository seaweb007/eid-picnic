Activities

Something for everyone

Eleven activity zones running from morning prayer to evening entertainment.

Explore the full lineup of activities — each designed to bring joy, connection, and unforgettable moments.

Games

Basketball, football, board games, and more — open to all skill levels.

Networking

Connect with professionals, creatives, and community leaders.

Sip & Paint

Unwind with a guided painting session — canvas, paints, and refreshments included.

Family Quiz

Test your knowledge in a fun, family-friendly quiz competition with prizes.

Kids Corner

Supervised play, face painting, crafts, and activities for little ones.

Entertainment

Live music, DJ sets, spoken word, and performances all day.

Food Court

Curated local vendors — from suya to jollof, small chops to drinks.

Photo Booth

Professional backdrops and props to capture your best moments.

Vendor Market

Explore unique products, crafts, and services from local entrepreneurs.

Giveaways

Exciting prizes, raffles, and surprises throughout the day.

Prayer Area

A quiet, dedicated space for prayer and reflection throughout the day.
