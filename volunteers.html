Volunteers

Volunteer With Us

Be part of the team that makes Eid Picnic happen. Gain experience, build community, and have fun.

Volunteer Roles

There's a place for everyone on our team.

Registration & Check-in

Welcome attendees, verify reservations, hand out welcome packages.

Kids Zone

Supervise activities, lead games, ensure kids have a safe, joyful experience.

Games & Activities

Organize and run sports, board games, and activities.

Photography & Content

Capture the day's best moments for our gallery and social media.

Vendor Support

Help vendors with setup, direct attendees, ensure smooth marketplace.

Cleanup & Logistics

Keep the venue clean, manage waste, help with teardown.

Responsibilities

  • Arrive on time for your assigned shift
  • Wear your volunteer identification
  • Communicate clearly with the volunteer coordinator
  • Represent Eid Picnic with kindness and professionalism

Volunteer Benefits

  • Free entry to Eid Picnic 2027
  • Volunteer certificate of participation
  • Meals and refreshments provided
  • Exclusive volunteer t-shirt

Ready to join the team?

We're looking for enthusiastic, reliable volunteers.
