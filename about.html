About Eid Picnic

Our Story · Our Community

Since 2020, we've been bringing Akure together to celebrate Eid in the most joyful way possible.

Who We Are

Celebrating together, connecting forever.

Eid Picnic is an annual community celebration organized by Jannah Immigrants. What started in 2020 as a small gathering of friends in a garden has grown into Akure's most anticipated Eid tradition.

Join Us This Year

Our Mission

To create a safe, joyful, and inclusive space where the Akure community can come together to celebrate Eid, strengthen bonds, and create lasting memories.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most beloved community Eid celebration — a model for how gatherings can build connection, culture, and belonging.

Our Journey

Seven years of memories

2020 · The Beginning

A small garden gathering of 50 friends and family — the seed of something beautiful.

2021 · Growing Roots

200+ attendees, first food vendors, and the introduction of the Kids Zone.

2022 · Building Community

500+ people, dedicated prayer area, and the first sip & paint session.

2023 · A New Home

Moved to Government House Gardens, 800+ attendees, full-day programming.

2024 · Going Big

1,200+ attendees, 10+ activity zones, first live entertainment stage.

2025 · The Gold Standard

1,800+ attendees, 20+ vendors, community awards and recognition.

2026 · Full Bloom

2,000+ attendees, expanded gallery, and the launch of the volunteer program.

2027 · 8th Edition

This year — bigger, better, and more community-focused than ever. We can't wait to welcome you.
