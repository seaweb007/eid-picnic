Become a Vendor at Eid Picnic 2027

Showcase your products, food, or services to thousands of attendees at Akure's biggest community Eid celebration.

Vendor Opportunities

From food to fashion, we welcome a diverse range of vendors.

Food Vendors

Sell delicious meals, snacks, and drinks to hungry attendees.

Product Vendors

Showcase clothing, accessories, crafts, and unique products.

Creative Vendors

Art, photography, calligraphy, and other creative services.

Registration Process

  1. Send us a message on WhatsApp expressing interest.
  2. We'll share available booth options and pricing.
  3. Confirm your spot and complete payment.
  4. Receive confirmation and vendor guidelines.
  5. Set up on the day and start selling!

Requirements

  • Valid food handling certification (for food vendors)
  • Your own equipment and table
  • Commitment to maintain a clean and professional booth
  • Arrive early for setup
